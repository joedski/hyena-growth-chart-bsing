Growth Chart

The values presented here are still a work in progress pending feedback and research.

The bars and left axis indicate the approximate body mass expected at a given age. The line and right axis indicate the linear growth, normalized to their expected maximum asymptotic growth; it is effectively to what percent their body has grown so far out of its total possible normal development. The body mass is derived from the unnormalized linear growth.

Age as indicated here is in months since birth, not conception. This is why the numbers don't start at 0.

Graph uses a monomolecular growth model BSed from this article in the Oxford Journal. May not be accurately interpreted. Most certainly being horribly abused.
